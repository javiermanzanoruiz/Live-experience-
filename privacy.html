1. Who we are

Live Experience ("we", "us") designs personalised sporting and lifestyle experiences in the south of Spain. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you use this website or submit an enquiry, why we collect it, and the rights you have over it.

2. What we collect

When you complete our enquiry form, we collect the information you choose to share, which may include: your name, country of residence, email address, phone number, preferred language, approximate travel dates, number of travellers, sporting preferences and level, accommodation preferences, dietary requirements, approximate budget, and any other details you include describing your ideal trip.

We do not knowingly collect any special category data (such as health information) beyond what you voluntarily disclose, for example a dietary restriction relevant to planning your stay.

3. Why we collect it

  • To respond to your enquiry and design a personalised travel proposal.
  • To communicate with you by email, phone or messaging services about your enquiry.
  • To keep a record of past enquiries and proposals, for our own operational and accounting purposes.

Our legal basis for this processing is your consent (given when you tick the consent box on the enquiry form) and, where a booking proceeds, the performance of a contract with you.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell personal data. We may share the minimum necessary information with:

  • Trusted partners directly involved in delivering your experience (for example, a hotel, a coach, or a restaurant), only once you have engaged us and only to the extent needed to arrange your trip.
  • Service providers who process data on our behalf, such as our form/email provider REVIEW: name the actual service, e.g. Formspree and any CRM or storage tool we use to manage enquiries.

5. International transfers

Because our clients come from Switzerland, the United Kingdom, the United States and across the EU/EEA, some of your data may be processed in a country outside your own. Where this happens outside the EU/EEA, we take reasonable steps to ensure it is protected to a comparable standard (for example, via standard contractual clauses where required).

6. How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data for as long as necessary to respond to you and, if you become a client, for the duration of our relationship plus any period required by law (for example, accounting obligations). 7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, to object to its processing, and to request a copy of it in a portable format. You may also withdraw consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before you did so.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@liveexperience.example. If you are in the EU/EEA and are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Cookies

This website does not currently use tracking or advertising cookies. It stores your selected display language locally in your browser (via localStorage) purely so the site remembers your preference; this is not shared with us or any third party.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@liveexperience.example.
