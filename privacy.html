Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

Live Experience is a newly-started project. This policy describes, honestly and plainly, what happens to your information today. It will be expanded once the business is formally registered.

1. Who this is

This website is currently operated by Javier Manzano Ruiz, the sports director of Live Experience, ahead of the business being formally incorporated.

2. What we collect

When you complete the enquiry form, we collect the information you choose to share: your name, country of residence, email address, phone number, preferred language, approximate travel dates, number of travellers, sporting preferences and level, approximate budget, and anything else you write describing your ideal trip.

We do not knowingly collect special category data (such as health information) beyond what you voluntarily mention, for example a note relevant to planning a training session.

3. Why we collect it

  • To read and personally respond to your enquiry.
  • To design a personalised proposal, if you would like one.
  • To keep a basic record of enquiries for our own reference.

The legal basis for this is your consent, given when you tick the consent box on the enquiry form.

4. Who sees it

We do not sell personal data. Your enquiry is sent, via the form on this site, to the email/inbox tool we use to receive enquiries, and is read personally by Javier. We do not share it with anyone else unless you go on to book an experience, at which point we would share only what is strictly necessary with the specific providers involved (for example, a golf course or club), and only with your knowledge.

5. International transfers

Because enquiries may come from Switzerland, the United Kingdom, the United States and across the EU/EEA, your data may be processed in a different country from where you live. Where this happens outside the EU/EEA, we take reasonable steps to keep it protected to a comparable standard.

6. How long we keep it

We keep enquiry data for as long as reasonably needed to respond to you, and for a limited period afterwards for our own reference.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, to object to its processing, and to receive a copy of it. You may withdraw consent at any time. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details on the homepage. If you are in the EU/EEA and are not satisfied with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

8. Cookies

This website does not use tracking or advertising cookies. It stores your chosen display language locally in your own browser (via localStorage) purely so the site remembers your preference; this never leaves your device.

9. Important note about bookings

Completing the enquiry form does not create a booking, a contract, or any obligation to pay. It simply starts a conversation.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent using the contact details on the homepage.
